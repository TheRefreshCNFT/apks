# CHOMPY

**Spin. Chomp. Survive!**

A fast-paced single-finger arcade game where you guide a cute hungry creature around a ring, devouring every morsel that flies outward from the center. Don't let food escape past you!

## How to Play

- **Drag** to spin Chompy around the outer ring
- **Eat** every food item that flies toward you from the center
- **Catch** golden stars for extra lives (starting Level 3)
- **Survive** 15 increasingly frantic levels of feeding frenzy!

## Features

- 15 levels of escalating chaos
- 10 different food types to devour
- Cute animated character with chomping face
- Fun crunchy sound effects and background beat
- Extra life power-ups from Level 3 onward
- Streak bonuses for consecutive catches
- Timer and eaten count tracking

## Build

```bash
chmod +x build-apk.sh
./build-apk.sh
```

Requires Android SDK (API 23) with `aapt`, `javac`, `dalvik-exchange`, `keytool`, and `jarsigner`.

## Package

`com.chompy.game` — Android 5.0+ (API 21)
